About Nakshatra

The Astronomy and Mathematics Society of NSUT

Who We Are

Nakshatra is a vibrant student-driven astronomy and space society dedicated to exploring the mysteries of the cosmos and fostering a deep appreciation for the universe among college students.

Our Mission

We aim to inspire curiosity, foster exploration, and promote astronomy through hands-on learning, collaboration, and community engagement.

Our Vision

We envision Nakshatra as a beacon of astronomical learning, inspiring future scientists while promoting scientific literacy and interdisciplinary research.
